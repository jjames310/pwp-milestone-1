Milestone 2

  • Content Strategy
      • My website will consist of 5 sections, welcome section, about me section, portfolio section, contact me section, and the footer section.
  • Welcome
      • My first section will be my name and a brief welcome text accompanied by a overlaying image.
  • About me
      • My second section will consist of my about me, which contains my personal achievements, a brief dive into life, description of my talents and abilities in order to show future employers reasons why they should hire me. It will be followed by a picture of myself to the side.
  • Portfolio
      • My third section will consist of a brief overview of some projects I have done with also a picture showing the completed project.
  • Contact Me
      • My fourth and final section will consist of a message board for an employer to fill out email,name,and message.
  • Footer
      • The very bottom of my page will be the footer which will include icons to social media
